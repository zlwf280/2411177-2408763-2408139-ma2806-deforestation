The Alarming Rate of Deforestation

In an era tainted by drastic, dire changes in our environment, our projectâ€™s aim is to shed light on this fluctuation, but through the lens of the unrelenting pace of deforestation that is changing our landscapes. Our research question is: How has the extent and nature of deforestation evolved over the last century, and will there be trends to help understand our future?

We are highlighting our blurred understanding of deforestation and its vast impact, attempting to elaborate on its importance through a mixture of engaging visualisation and written theoretical text. Through an in-depth graphical analysis of deforestation trends during the last century, we want to decipher the intricate story of environmental degradation and its global ramifications.

This project is not just an effort for the sake of academics, but rather is an investigation of a misunderstood phenomena by those passionate about its cause, and a call to action that invites audiences to reevaluate our individual and collective environmental policy and motivate activities that support a more ecologically aware and sustainable global community.
